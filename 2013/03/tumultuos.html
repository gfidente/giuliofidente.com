Tumultuos

A tumultuous post, written on a rush and with many external references... but worth sharing I thought.

While working to port (or reinit) this blog over to Pelican, we in Italy had the elections. Not a word that I know describes how disgusted I am to learn that I share this country with a 25% circa of people who still voted for Berlusconi.

At least I found a good documentary (one more?) on this "Italian times" and it is called Girlfriend in a Coma. Most of it is discussed by Bill Emmott and that is indeed what makes it so interesting. I share his view on the actual result coming from the elections. I suggest you to read his article on the Independent also to get a meaningful wrap up on the status quo, it includes comments for both Bersani and Grillo indeed.

And I'm not finished yet with the changes, I'm also getting my hands dirty on OpenStack (starting with Cinder and Tempest)! Things at RHOS are going well and it is good fun to work on this stuff, plus the block volumes management in the cloud really is area of big challenges. To my joyness.

Talk to you soon.
